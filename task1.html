India Introduces New Digital Learning Initiative

Date: 25 October 2025 |

The Government of India has launched a new digital learning initiative aimed at modernising classrooms across the country. This initiative will provide students access to smart-classroom infrastructure and digital learning materials.

Officials say that this programme will equip students with skills relevant for the 21st century workforce. Training sessions for teachers are also planned to ensure effective implementation of the new system.

Key Features of the Initiative:

  • Provision of tablets and smart boards in schools.
  • Free access to digital textbooks and online learning portals.
  • Teacher training on technology-enhanced pedagogy.
  • Regional pilot projects before nationwide rollout.

More details and updates on timelines and eligibility criteria are expected to be announced by the Ministry of Education later this year.
